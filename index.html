🩺 Medical Colleges in Uttarakhand

Top MBBS/BDS Institutes List

Uttarakhand offers premium medical education. To secure a seat, students must qualify for **NEET UG**. Counseling is conducted by HNB Medical University.

  • AIIMS Rishikesh: Premier Central Institute. Admission via NEET.
  • GMC Haldwani: Established in 1997. Government College with low fees.
  • Doon Medical College: Located in Dehradun. Top clinical exposure.
  • VCSG Srinagar (Garhwal): Highly reputed govt college in the hills.
  • SGRRMC & Himalayan (HIHT): Top Private Medical Options in Dehradun.

🏗️ Engineering Colleges in Uttarakhand

Top B.Tech/B.E. Institutes List

Uttarakhand mein Engineering ke liye top government aur private options hain. Admission ke liye **JEE Mains** exam sabse zaroori hai. Uttarakhand Technical University (UTU) counseling manage karti hai.

  • IIT Roorkee: India ka sabse purana engineering college. Admission via **JEE Advanced**.
  • NIT Uttarakhand (Srinagar): Top placement records. Admission via **JEE Mains** (JOSAA Counseling).
  • GB Pant University (Pantnagar): Agriculture aur Engineering ke liye world-famous. Iska apna entrance exam hota hai.
  • THDC-IHET (Tehri): Tehri mein sthit Govt institute jo Hydro Engineering ke liye best hai.
  • Graphic Era & DIT University: Dehradun ke top private engineering options jinme achhi placements milti hain.

🎨 Career Options: Arts & Commerce

Best Courses & Colleges After 12th

Science ke alawa Uttarakhand mein Arts aur Commerce ke students ke liye world-class institutes hain. Admission ke liye **CUET UG** aur **CLAT** jaise exams dene hote hain.

1. Hotel Management (BHM)

Best College: IHM Dehradun (Institute of Hotel Management).
Exam: NCHMCT JEE (National Level).
Scope: World-wide Hotels, Airlines aur Cruise ships mein career.

IHM Dehradun Official Site 🔗

2. BBA / B.Com / Eco Hons

Best Colleges: Doon University, HNB Garhwal University.
Exam: CUET UG (Common University Entrance Test).
Specialization: Finance, Marketing aur Human Resource.

Apply via CUET 🔗

3. Law (BA LLB / BBA LLB)

Best Colleges: Law College Dehradun (LCD), Uttaranchal University.
Exam: CLAT (Common Law Admission Test) ya LSAT.
Duration: 5 Years Integrated Course.
